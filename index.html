Dr. Grace Sidberry

Dr. Grace Sidberry is a licensed, clinical psychologist who has been in private practice for over 30 years. Her lifelong interest in studying psychology and working to help others overcome emotional issues began while she was growing up in Jamaica.

Dr. Sidberry received an undergraduate degree in Psychology from Texas Wesleyan College, Fort Worth, Texas. After completing an internship with Baylor College of Medicine in Houston, Texas she earned her Ph.D. degree in Clinical Psychology from the University of North Texas, Denton, Texas.

In addition to her clinical work in psychology, Dr. Sidberry taught at Florida International University and Barry University as an adjunct professor. She has also served as a consultant for the United States Job Corps.

Dr. Sidberry integrates insights from the field of neuroscience and mind body medicine to help her clients attain wellness in mind, body, emotions and spirit. She uses treatment strategies which are research based and which provide practical tools for overcoming challenges.
